about

what we can offer


The Station offers one of the best destinations in the Eastern Sierras for groups, weddings, meetings, and conferences. Along with a 360 degree view of Mammoth Lakes, we offer a wide variety of programs for your group to participate in. Whether you're interested in a snowboarding camp, backpacking trip or even organizing a leadership retreat we can accommodate your group with the perfect facilities and programming as well as experienced guides. Take a look below to view some of the many different programs we offer. Whatever outdoor activity program you're looking for, we can definitely take care of!

Food & Lodging

what to expect


There is nothing worse than rock climbing or hiking after a bad night’s sleep, on a poor diet or both. We do our best to make sure campers are fully rested and fed so they can go all out the next day. Our comfortable cabins come furnished with plush mattresses, forced air heating and full bathrooms. As well, our camp chef has been heralded by many past campers as their favorite staff member. Campers will feel like they are eating home-cooked meals even if they are hours away from mom and dad. Nothing is better after a day outdoors than amazing comfort food!﻿
