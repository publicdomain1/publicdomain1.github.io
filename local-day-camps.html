About The Program

Hey, Eastern Sierra Locals! Have no place to leave your kids during the summer while you're at the office? Would you rather have them surrounded by professional, adult supervision than someone down the street? What about having them be active rather than staying at home playing Xbox? Well, your questions have just been answered. For just $12 a day, The Station is offering local day camps for kids between the ages of 6 and 12! From 9am until 3pm, we'll hang out, eat lunch, play games and make sure your kids have an amazing summer. Local activities include arts/crafts, games, kayaking, and more! Kids can sign up for the entire week or just individual days.
