About

The Station is a non-profit youth camp whose mission is to see the lives of today’s youth forever changed and never the same. We seek to transform and empower these individuals through their encounter and experience with the outdoors by facilitating action sports and recreational activities such as snowboarding and rock climbing.

We want these individuals to experience the outdoors in a way they never have before. Rather than serving just as a lodging facility and having youth come up here for a quick weekend trip, we want them to take away a feeling of accomplishment and renewal, filled with new life skills that they can use, implement and influence their peers with.﻿

INTERNSHIP

join our team


believers often come across really familiar questions. Is Jesus beckoning you to follow Him? Are you up for the challenge? If you follow Him, what does that look like? How might following Him change your life right now? Do you believe that you can have a great influence in the next generation of youth?


Well, here is your chance to have all those questions answered. The Station internship program provides a launching pad for young people to figure out and step into the calling that God has on their lives. Through leadership training, job shadowing, and hands on ministry at each of our camps, The Station interns have an opportunity to take action in their walk with God. Interns are challenged spiritually, mentally and physically each day. Ministry is not easy, and this next generation needs leaders that will last so that the truth of Jesus Christ can be spread across the world. Our heart is to raise up leaders that will persevere in the purpose and destiny that God has for their lives.
