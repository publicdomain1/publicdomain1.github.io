About The Program

During our ministry camps, we like to make sure all of our core values are highlighted – God’s presence, the experience, healing and equipping. We want The Station to not only be a place where kids can have a good time snowboarding, rock climbing and playing games, but as a place where they encounter the Holy Spirit in a way they never have before. We want to be sure the activities and experiences they have help them overcome any type of struggle they may be facing. Our goal is to show campers the love of God and the hope of His salvation. Lastly, we want to be sure that we are preparing and equipping the next generation of believers to stand strongly and boldly in their faith, speaking life and love to those on and off the hill.

Food & Lodging

what to expect


There is nothing worse than rock climbing or hiking after a bad night’s sleep, on a poor diet or both. We do our best to make sure campers are fully rested and fed so they can go all out the next day. Our comfortable cabins come furnished with plush mattresses, forced air heating and full bathrooms. As well, our camp chef has been heralded by many past campers as their favorite staff member. Campers will feel like they are eating home-cooked meals even if they are hours away from mom and dad. Nothing is better after a day outdoors than amazing comfort food!﻿
