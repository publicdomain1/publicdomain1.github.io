About The Program

Looking to reach that Division 1 level with your team or break personal records? Then let us host your high altitude training! In our experience, we've seen runners go to training camps but get burnt out not because of their training but because they have other things to worry about - buying and cooking food, sleeping on uncomfy mattresses and being thrown into crowded facilities. At The Station, all your runners have to do is worry about one thing - RUNNING. We'll take care of the rest! This includes balanced meals, comfortable cabins and sleeping arrangements and team-building activities and games.

Food & Lodging

what to expect


There is nothing worse than rock climbing or hiking after a bad night’s sleep, on a poor diet or both. We do our best to make sure campers are fully rested and fed so they can go all out the next day. Our comfortable cabins come furnished with plush mattresses, forced air heating and full bathrooms. As well, our camp chef has been heralded by many past campers as their favorite staff member. Campers will feel like they are eating home-cooked meals even if they are hours away from mom and dad. Nothing is better after a day outdoors than amazing comfort food!﻿
